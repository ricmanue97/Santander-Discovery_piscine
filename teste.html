Clark Kent

Journalist & Kryptonian

Clark Kent, known as Superman, is an alien from the planet Krypton with extraordinary powers. Raised on Earth, he upholds justice and truth while living undercover as a mild-mannered reporter in Metropolis. His strength, flight, and unwavering moral compass make him Earth's greatest protector.
